Dining Plan Locations

These are the meal locations listed on Carowinds' dining plan page, with the park area and the typical food each spot serves.

Beach Bites

Waterpark quick stop

Carolina Harbor
🎢 Ricochet
🍤 Seafood + snacks: Fish and chips, shrimp and chips, fries, pretzels, and soft-serve.

Blue Ridge Country Kitchen

Carolina comfort food

Blue Ridge Junction
🎢 Copperhead Strike
🍗 Chicken + comfort: Roasted chicken, tenders, wings, sausage, salads, and Southern sides.

Burrito Café

Mexican-inspired fast casual

Celebration Plaza
🎢 Vortex
🌯 Mexican: Burritos, burrito salads, loaded nachos, taco salad, walking tacos, chips, and salsa.

Camp Cookout

Kid-friendly classics

Camp Snoopy
🎢 Kiddy Hawk
🍔 Kids' classics: Tenders, cheeseburgers, hot dogs, fries, mini corn dogs, and kids' meals.

Chickie's & Pete's Sports Grill

Sports bar break

Carolina Boardwalk
🎢 Carolina Goldrusher
🍟 Grill + wings: Cheesesteaks, Crabfries, wings, fried shrimp, Caesar salad, and nachos.

Frequent Fryers

Aeronautica fried favorites

Aeronautica Landing
🎢 Afterburn
🍗 Fried bites: Popcorn chicken, shrimp, chips, and fries.

Harbor House

Flagship waterpark meal stop

Carolina Harbor
🎢 Ricochet
🍖 BBQ + burgers: Tenders, pulled pork, fried shrimp, ribs, burgers, wraps, fruit, and salads.

Harmony Hall

Largest dining hall in the park

Celebration Plaza
🎢 Hurler
🍕 Variety hall: BBQ, pizza, poke bowls, grilled cheese, salads, burgers, and tenders.

Juke Box Diner

Retro diner meal

Thrill Zone
🎢 Hurler
🍔 Diner fare: Burgers, chicken tenders, roast beef sandwiches, onion rings, fries, and fruit.

Leonardo's Hometown Italian Food

Pasta and pizza stop

Aeronautica Landing
🎢 Afterburn
🍕 Italian: Pizza, pasta, stuffed shells, chicken parmesan or sausage piadinas, and salads.

Nacho Nook

Quick nacho stop

Thunder Road Area
🎢 Hurler
🧀 Nachos: Chicken tinga nachos and pork carnitas nachos.

Panda Express

Reliable stir-fry option

Camp Snoopy Edge
🎢 Kiddy Hawk
🥡 Chinese: Orange chicken, kung pao chicken, broccoli beef, and combo plates.

Papa Luigi's

Classic pizza counter

Carousel Park
🎢 Vortex
🍕 Pizza: Cheese and pepperoni pizza, specialty pizza, breadsticks, salads, and meatball subs.

Sharky's Grille

Waterpark family meal stop

Carolina Harbor
🎢 Kiddy Hawk
🍔 Family favorites: Chicken tenders, cheeseburgers, jumbo hot dogs, cheese pizza, pepperoni pizza, and fries.
